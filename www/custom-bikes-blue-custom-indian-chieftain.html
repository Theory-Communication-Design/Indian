BLUE CUSTOM INDIAN CHIEFTAIN

JUNE 14 2014

Never underestimate the value of being prepared, that's what we always say. And judging by the looks of two very special Chevrolet Silverado concepts that made their public debut at the State Fair of Texas recently, their designers at Chevrolet agree.

The first of these two striking trucks, the Silverado Z71 Volunteer Firefighter concept, pays homage to the men and women who serve as first responders all across the country. The Silverado Double Cab boasts a "full complement of rescue equipment" in addition to the Z71 Off Road suspension.

The second of these two, the fierce-looking Black Ops concept, based on the Silverado Crew Cab 4x4, is not only incredibly cool looking, but has "features designed to turn the wasteland into an oasis, including custom-fabricated lower body armor and a lockable, bed-mounted Truck Vault storage unit."
