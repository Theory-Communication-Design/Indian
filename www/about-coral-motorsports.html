Coral Motorsports of Pompano Beach is proud to be the newest member of the motorcycle community in Florida.

We are your full-service, full-line Indian motorcycle and product dealership. At Coral Motorsports of Pompano Beach, we are poised to be the premier destination for all things motorcycle.

Parts, service, Indian apparel, and accessories are just the beginning. Each month we will host an event at our store for the community to come together in celebration of the iconic heritage of the Indian Motorcycle.

Live local groups and musicians will perform on our stage. Quality vendors, authentic BBQ, door prizes and giveaways will be in abundant supply. Special sale dates will be posted to allow the purchase of our one of a kind apparel and Indian Motorcycle T-Shirts.

Demo events will be ongoing throughout the year, allowing any person to experience first-hand the awesomeness of the Indian Motorcycle.

Displays will document the history and the heritage of Indian Motorcycles starting from the birth of America's first motorcycle in 1901.

A photo opportunity area will be available to create that special lifetime memory of your visit to our unique motorcycle destination to hang in your home, whether it is right here in Florida, anywhere around our nation, or in your country, documenting your visit to Coral Motorsports of Pompano Beach, Florida USA.
